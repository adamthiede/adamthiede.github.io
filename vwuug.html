Unix User Group for Vancouver, Washington, USA


A Linux User Group exists in Portland, and they're Unix-Friendly, but I'd like this one to be more inclusive of Unix/BSD. Also, for folks in Vancouver, traveling into Portland can be a hassle during the week.

Not only does "Unix and Unix-like" encompass Linux as well, but I belive the BSDs and modern Unixes are more technically interesting than Linux. Hopefully this can be a group of like-minded Unix users that can gather, discuss what we are up to, learn about tools, share ideas, and make a few friends.

Community service and outreach should be an important aspect of this group. While it would be great to talk amongst ourselves, making our geographic region better by helping them with problems or introducing them to new concepts is going to be another big focus of this group.

Seminars, round-tables, town halls, whatever you want to call them - we need a way to share our knowledge with the public so that they can better understand the confusing landscape of modern technology themselves, too.

I'm going to start reaching out to places like the libraries and the newspapers, to see if this is something they're interested in promoting, or helping host.
