This is going to be a difficult journey. It will require changing habits and changing yourlifestyle. There might be some crazy people who get mad at you, becuase you don't want to use Facebook. That's OK. It's important to make your own decisions and stand by your principles. Liberating yourself from digital slavery is the right thing to do, and no one can fault you for wanting more freedom, independence, and privay in your life.

Big Tech is getting into the home security game. Security systems that you buy and use should be made by a company that is concerned with making security systems, like SimpliSafe, ADT, or Brinks. Companies like Google have an impressive track record of killing off business units, and that's not what you want regarding home security.

Other modern "Internet of Things" gadgets are a no-go. If you're creeped out by your smartphone listening to you, Ring doorbell cameras surveilling every neighborhood, and Amazon's Alexa doing whatever it does, IoT gadgets are even worse. Don't put these in your house and on your network.

If you're buying audio gadgets, like bluetooth speakers or headphones, make sure that they're just that - speakers and headphones. Anything that requires a smartphone app to control keeps you tethered to Big Tech's ball-and-chain.

If you're confused, and if this seems all too much, send me and email and I'll help you out personally. I'm personally invested in the independence and freedom of my friends and family and enjoy using the skills and knowledge I have to help others out.
