Great Movie

[link—standalone]

Last night I watched an amazing movie. The plot was structured pretty well, the pacing was spot-on, the characters were human and believable. You should watch it. It was fantastic, and I loved it. I rarely give a movie or show such a high recommendation, but this was beautiful.

Since many people might be put off by things like this, two things out of the way - it's an anime movie, and it's called "I want to eat your pancreas".

But don't worry - no cannibalism. Just a lovely film abot two humans dealing with the meaning of life, mortality, and the importance of human relationships. There's more info here, on its MAL page. But do yourself a favor - don't watch trailers, don't look at reviews, summaries, whatever - just sit down with it. It's important that the various plot points aren't spoiled for you, as with every movie. Be respectful of the creator - just wait for them to come up in the film.

I can't recommend it enough. Do yourself a favor and watch it.

by Adam Thiede
