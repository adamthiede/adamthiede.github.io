If you want to give me a gift, please do so according to the following guidelines. I am quite particular about goods that I use.


  • First and foremost, follow the list. This is an Amazon (ugh, I know) wishlist that I personally add items to.
  • I like books, especially technical books. I prefer physical books, but DRM-free ePub files are also a lovely gift.
  • Gift cards are good, but cash is better. If you direct me how to spend it (in abstract) I will abide by the suggestion, i.e. "Use it for coffee" or "Buy your wife some art supplies".
  • If you're at a loss and don't like the idea of giving someone cash (I understand!) beer is good too. I'll always be thankful for some strange craft stout.
  • I have enough clothing, am particular about how clothing fits, and my body is not the size or shape you think it is. I will deal with this myself.
  • I like computers and "computer stuff", but again, am very particular. I will do it myself.
  • A notable exception is Thinkpads, MacBooks, or desktop systems at least 10 years old - if you have an old computer that is physically clean, (I will not accept something caked in gunk) I will be overjoyed to play with it.
