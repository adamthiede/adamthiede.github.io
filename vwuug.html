Unix/Linux User Group for Vancouver, Washington, USA


I'm looking to bring together a group of like-minded individuals interested in the following:

  • Free Software
  • Open Source
  • Linux, Unix, BSDs
  • Self-Hosting
  • system administration
  • programming, scripting, hacking
  • virtualization, networking
  • devops, cloud

If you'd like to sharpen your skills, help your community grow, and make a few friends, this group is for you.


Scheduled Meetings: 6-8PM on Wednesday and 6-8PM on Saturday (for now)

Video/audio chat on jitsi is optional. These hours will change and meetings will likely be once or twice a month once I get more people's schedules figured out.

Talk on Matrix: #vwuug:libera.chat (this room is bridged with IRC)

Talk on IRC:#vwuug on libera.chat



I'm aware of PLUG and am not trying to steal their thunder or members. This is targeted at Vancouver residents who want to stay on this side of the river for various reasons - travel time, parking, local support, etc.
