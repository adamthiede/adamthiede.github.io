There's been a lot going around for the past few years about MacBook {Pro,Air} keyboards and how awful they are. Saw something again today that made me put it in writing.

I was a Mac enthusiast until it came time to swap my busted-up 2013 Macbook Air for something with more RAM. I actually went all in on Thinkpads+Linux because of the horror of those keyboards back in 2016. Since then Apple's seemed to be taking more than they're giving. Good riddance. Glad I left.

Just like last time - stop hitting yourself! This company wants your money and nothing more. Just leave it and go for something that'll respect you as a person. Go with a used machine - a Dell Latitude or Lenovo Thinkpad (business laptops built to last) that can have all their parts replaced and have actual service manuals!

And while you're at it, chuck Ubuntu on there or something. ElementaryOS is really pretty if you like mac stuff, and KDE Neon is super functional and customizable. 

Open Source / Free Software at least isn't trying to hurt you. Maybe start there. It won't solve everything but it'll be pretty damn close.
