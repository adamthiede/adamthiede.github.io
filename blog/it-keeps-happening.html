The technocracy only gets worse and worse!

This is a story about some dads who were concerned for their young sons' welfare. In two separate and unrelated cases, a dad took pictures of his naked son to send to doctors for a diagnosis; in 2020/1, in-person doctor visits had to be for emergencies. Shortly after, the dad's email, photo backup, chat, and phone stopped working. Thanks Google! The police even went after the poor concerned fathers. (Of course they were both innocent.)

A Google spokeswoman said the company stands by its decisions, even though law enforcement cleared the two men. This is an autocratic tone-deaf no-forgiveness policy written by people who have no children. Apple attempted to do the same exact thing last year, but there was a much bigger outcry against it, so they didn't do it. (Or maybe they did. We will never know. iCloud is non-free and the code is private to Apple.)

Stop using Apple and Google, right now. These companies will stop at nothing to enforce policies that harm innocent people, invade privacy, censor and modify communications, rewrite history, and ultimately change how people think and act. Their goal is control over the world's information supply. (If it's not, well then they've done a good job of stumbling into it on accident, haven't they?) There is no forgiveness or humanity here, just a ruthless authority staring over your shoulder at everything you say and do, ready to pounce.
