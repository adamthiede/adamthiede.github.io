Welcome to the year 2019.

It's going to be a good year. The common trend is for people to catastrophize, and say that things are going to get worse (as they kind of have been for the past few years), but that's not the only option. You can choose to be positive, and embrace the unknown. Really, people, most of us are the masters of our own destinies. There are limitations to everything in life - moving is hard, getting into a new job is difficult for many people, and there's no guarantee that any change will make your life better!

But you know, if you're reading this, chances are you're not in prison, and you likely do have some sort of sustainable income. You can make the changes that you want. I don't know who you are, but I belive in you! 

2018 was, for me, quite a terrible year. Probably the worst so far. But, in 2018, I got a raise at work, I moved into a beautiful house in a neighborhood that I love, I corebooted my Thinkpad x220, I ran hundreds of miles, and I read a lot more than 2017. Despite being a bad year, it was also a good year.

Even if it's been a bad year, remember to look at all the good things that happened too, and to be thankful.
