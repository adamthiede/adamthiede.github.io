My friends,

I feel like I should write something for you, because you deserve a parting gift, and this is all I can think to give. You've given me so much to be grateful for the time we've played together.

You all played your characters remarkably well, and they are all well thought out and interesting characters who brought so much depth and delight to our sessions.

Sifting through my memories, a few memorable moments stand out:

  • Johan's slow slide from gentle peacekeeper and Good Boy to vengeful bloodthirsty bodybuilder
  • Johan and Toad breaking into an opposing team's castle and caused havoc in disguise
  • In an early session where not all of us could play, Rowan and Mathias launched toad into the air to take down a dragon mid-flight
  • Bug ending combat early with surprise massive explosions (the caravan on our way to the city, and the pirate ships filled with pirates we'd barely been introduced to that Nathaniel had made a whole background for)
  • Archimedes eating rocks
  • Family bonding for both Rowan and Bug
  • Breadsticks, pamphlets, chocolate, and multiple different pet snakes (RIP)
  • The blood fountain that we just kind of ignored until it got way worse
  • Multiple times Mathias fell for mimic traps
  • Our party's widening ideological differences as time went on (it was not frustrating - it made things much funnier!)
  • Chadicus getting in our way and getting what he deserved
  • Rowan getting shot, posioned, possessed, etc. (Mathias is the DM's in-combat punching bag and Rowan is the out-of-combat punching bag!)
  • The first time we explored Darwick's estate our animal companions destroyed the property, and we convinced the gardener it was his fault somehow

And of course, I can't commend Nathaniel enough for being an impressive host. Your switching between many different characters' voices and personalities is difficult enough, but you do it all while crafting a believable world with so much to discover; there was always something prepared for us around every corner. We had several guests/temporary party members that did a wonderful job integrating with us and added so much extra flavor to the sessions. Rigo's multiple appearances were always colorful and fun, Ryan's automaton character was out of this world and completely unexpected, and our last guest whose name I don't remember (sorry!) did a beautiful job paying off the Vera hype. I didn't cry!

While I'm grateful for seeing a pleasant conclusion to the overarching story, I didn't feel like I needed to - it wasn't about the destination, it was more about the friends we made along the way. We could've gone on for much longer without reaching the end and I would've been perfectly content, because there was always a new fun way Nathaniel would come up with for the Irregulars to get into trouble. Every session was a blast, even though the early ones were a bit awkward sometimes. This was a highlight of my life for so long, and something I always looked forward to.

Anyway, I could go on for a while, but I'll stop here. I wish you luck in your next adventure. If I find a local group to join, they'll have to clear the high bar you all have set.

Be well, and play on.
