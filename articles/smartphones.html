So you want a smartphone, but:

- you don't like Apple or Google's tracking,

- you don't want to get scammed,

- you don't see any options?

I've got your back. Here is a list. (Or maybe you want a dumbphone instead?)

Easy Mode

Buy one of these:

  1. Join the Calyx Institute, and get a phone with CalyxOS installed. It's the most usable privacy-conscious Android replacaement OS.
  2. Buy a Murena device from /e/ foundation. Also a highly usable device that respects your privacy.
  3. Buy a NitroPhone from NitroKey. They come with GrapheneOS. It's a little less user friendly but much more secure and private.

Slightly Less Easy Mode

Buy a Google Pixel [1] or other supported device [2], send it to me, and let me install CalyxOS, GrapheneOS, LineageOS, or DivestOS on it.
Alternatively, I can buy it, install the OS, and send it to you. I'll do it for free.

Hard Mode

The same as the option above, but you install it yourself. The instructions are difficult for Lineage, but easy for Calyx/Graphene, and on Pixels, it's easy to recover from any screw-ups.

Budget Mode

The same as the option above, but you dig through your house, ask neighbors, or beg friends and family to see if they have any 2-to-10-year-old Android phones on any of the supported devices lists. [2] Then you install it yourself or ask me to do it.

Patriotism Mode

You want something made in the USA? That's your one option, and it's a very good option. I don't have one because I can't afford it.

Getting a privacy-respecting smartphone is very easy. Chances are, you live in a place where you can get one of the smartphones with CalyxOS, GrapheneOS, or LineageOS. If not, you can always find a used device on Swappa [3] or eBay [4].
