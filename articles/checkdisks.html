How to figure out what filesystem a disk is formatted in


You likely found this page from the filesystem format page.


Operating SystemTextScreenshot
WindowsOpen Windows Explorer.
Right-click the disk.
Click Properties.
macOSOpen Finder.
Right-click, or control-click, your disk and select Get Info.
LinuxOpen Disks.
Select your disk.
If you don't have Disks, run df -Th in a terminal.
