Great Movie

[link—standalone]

Last night I watched an amazing movie. The plot was structured pretty well, the pacing was spot-on, the characters were human and believable. You should watch it. It was fantastic, and I loved it. I rarely give a movie or show such a high recommendation, but this was beautiful.

Since many people might be put off by things like this, two things out of the way - it's an anime movie, and it's called "I want to eat your pancreas".

But don't worry - no cannibalism. Just a lovely film abot two humans dealing with the meaning of life, mortality, and the importance of human relationships. There's more info here, on its MAL page. If you feel like watching it, as with every movie, do your best to avoid spoilers, summaries, etc. beforehand.

If you don't mind tearjerkers, this is a good one.

by Adam Thiede
