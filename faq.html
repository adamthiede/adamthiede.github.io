About Me / FAQ


  • Who are you? What is this site?
  • I'm Adam Thiede! This web presence is important these days since everyone needs to own a little slice of the Internet. Websites are much better than social media, and very easy to make.

  • Why is this website so ugly?
  • Since I'm a SysAdmin I had to do it myself! And since I'm a SysAdmin and not a full-stack web developer, It's not using any sort of fancy modern technologies, but it works better than most of the sites that are. It's just a few HTML files on a tiny web server. No trendy frameworks, no JavaScript, no weird dependencies, and I wrote it all by hand. Simplicity is beautiful, and I'm saving you data and battery life.

  • Any interesting facts, hobbies, or information about yourself you can share?
  • I love to read (and write!), I'm a collector of old laptops, I have a gym in my garage, and my favorite color is purple.
