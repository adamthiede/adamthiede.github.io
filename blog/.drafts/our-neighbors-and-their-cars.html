A local household meme

In December I became unemployed, and in March I started working again. I've been working from home every day (which is awesome, by the way) and my office room faces the street. This give me the opportunity to track my neighbors' behavior regarding their cars.

Before this sounds too weird, hear me out - the amount that these people get in their cars just to drive for what seems like 10 minutes is too damn high! They have three cars of their own, and three regular visitors (who are likely relatives). Frequently the cars will come and go multiple times an hour, just gone for about 10-30 minutes, and then come right back. Frequently they will also get in the cars just to park them in different locations. It's mind-boggling.
